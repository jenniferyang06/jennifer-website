Online Appointment System

The online appointment system for the Barangay of Lemon in Butuan City is a convenient and efficient platform that allows residents to schedule appointments for various government services. Through an easy-to-use website or mobile app, residents can select their desired service, choose a preferred date and time, and receive confirmation details. This system reduces waiting times, streamlines operations, and improves the overall citizen experience, making it easier for residents to access necessary administrative tasks.
